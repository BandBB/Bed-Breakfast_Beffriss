Fun Places and Activities Near Beffriss B&B

Niagara Falls Boat Tour

Get up close and personal with the breathtaking Niagara Falls on a thrilling boat tour. The Maid of the Mist boat tours take you directly under the falls for an unforgettable experience. The boat tour dock is just a 10-minute walk from Beffriss B&B.

Skylon Tower Observation Deck

Enjoy a panoramic view of the majestic falls and the surrounding landscape from the top of the Skylon Tower, which is only a 5-minute drive away from the B&B.

Niagara Falls State Park

Explore the natural beauty of Niagara Falls State Park, where you can hike, picnic, and enjoy the scenic views. This park is only 15 minutes away by car and offers a peaceful retreat from the busy tourist areas.

Clifton Hill - The Street of Fun

Clifton Hill is filled with fun attractions such as arcades, wax museums, mini-golf, and more! It's just a short 10-minute walk from Beffriss B&B, making it perfect for a day of family-friendly fun.
